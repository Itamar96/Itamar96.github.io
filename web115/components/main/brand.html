Brand Guide

General

Summary

Iridescent Cinderwyrm is a massage and bodywork practice founded by Itamar Castillo, dedicated to holistic wellness, relaxation, and expressive healing. The brand blends artistry and care, offering clients a restorative experience that emphasizes balance, accessibility, and personal identity.
