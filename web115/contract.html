Concept and Mission

Aethernova is a personal brand focused on software craftsmanship and learning, where clear communication meets elegant engineering. The brand leans into an aurora aesthetic: deep night backdrops with luminous accents to symbolize discovery and momentum.

Usage Rules

  • Background: gradient from Midnight Plum to Surface Grape to Space Navy.
  • Text: Starlight (#E6E6F0) on dark backgrounds. Increase contrast on small text.
  • Links and Buttons: Aurora Violet (#C084F5) with hover glow. Avoid pure white unless needed.
  • Headings: Montserrat, bold, with controlled glow. Keep paragraph glow minimal.
  • Imagery: soft rounded corners. Optional gradient borders for hero or portraits.
