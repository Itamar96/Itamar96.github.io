Brand Guide

Brand name: Iridescent Cinderwyrm Bodywork
Tagline: Breathe. Unwind. Renew.

Concept and Mission

Iridescent Cinderwyrm Bodywork offers massage and relaxation services that focus on calm, comfort, and intentional relief. The Desert Matcha palette uses warm sand, herbal greens, and a hint of clay to feel natural and restorative. The voice is simple, warm, and professional.

Usage Rules & Quick Overview

  • Background: sand base with a subtle matcha and clay wash
  • Headings: Playfair Display with clear hierarchy and calm punctuation
  • Links and Buttons: links use #3E574B, primary buttons use #6F8F6B with white text
  • Imagery: rounded corners, soft shadows, and natural light when possible
  • Tone: calm, clear, and reassuring
  • Structure: header, main, footer with a consistent menu
  • Accessibility: good contrast, focus outlines, reduced motion support
  • Branding: Desert Matcha palette with gold accents, Playfair Display headings, Inter body, soft glow
