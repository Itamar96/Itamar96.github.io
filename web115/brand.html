Concept and Mission

Aethernova Bodywork offers massage and relaxation services that focus on calm, comfort, and intentional relief. The brand uses soft neutrals, eucalyptus greens, and gentle lavender to signal trust and relaxation. The voice is simple, warm, and professional.

Color Palette

  • #F7F3EFLinen background
  • #FFFFFFSurface and cards
  • #263238Primary text
  • #2E7D6DAccent, buttons
  • #1F5A50Links and small text
  • #7BAE9CSoft accent
  • #C7B8D6Muted lavender

Contrast checks: text #263238 on #F7F3EF is accessible for body copy. White text on #2E7D6D meets contrast for buttons. For small inline links on linen, use #1F5A50 for stronger contrast.
