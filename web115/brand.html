Brand Guide

Standards for the Iridescent Cinderwyrm Bodywork identity: color, typography, logo usage, and voice.

Color Palette

  • Deep Pine — #0F3B2E
  • Warm Sand — #C5B59C
  • Iridescent Violet — #B68AFF
  • Ember Gold — #FFD271
  • Ink — #111111

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display (700–800).
Body/UI: Inter (400–600).

Use generous line-height for readability. Avoid more than two sizes per section.

Logo & Icon

Brand Voice

  • Grounded: evidence-based wellness, avoid pseudoscience.
  • Warm: calm, welcoming tone; second-person (“you”).
  • Precise: concise copy, clear actions.

Usage Examples

Buttons: Ember Gold background, Ink text, 12px radius.

Links: Iridescent Violet; underline on hover/focus; visible focus ring.
