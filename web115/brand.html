Brand Guide

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display — elegant, high-contrast serif for titles.

Body: Inter — modern, clean sans-serif for readability.

Dark Theme Palette (opt-in)

  • #1E1E1E Deep background
  • #202020 Panels / surfaces
  • #EAEAEA Text (on dark)
  • #88BFA2 Accent (dark)
  • #64A083 Accent deep (dark)

Color Palette (Desert Matcha)

  • #F7EAD9 Sand background
  • #FFFFFF Surface and cards
  • #2F3A34 Primary text
  • #6F8F6B Accent buttons
  • #3E574B Links and small text
  • #8DA3A6 Mist (subtle UI)
  • #C4633A Clay glow
  • #FFF8DC Holiday light background
  • #D4AF37 Gold accent

Contrast: #2F3A34 on #F7EAD9 passes for body copy. White text on #6F8F6B passes on buttons.
